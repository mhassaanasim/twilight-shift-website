Project Overview

Twilight Shift is an innovative 3D platformer that challenges players to navigate between two interconnected realms: Dawn and Dusk. Built in Godot, the game features a unique realm-shifting mechanic where players must seamlessly transition between the bright, serene Dawn realm (white atmosphere) and the mysterious, intense Dusk realm (red atmosphere) to complete levels. Platforms essential for progression are strategically scattered across both realms, requiring players to master mid-air realm switching to solve puzzles and make impossible jumps. This dual-reality gameplay creates a dynamic puzzle-platforming experience where timing, spatial awareness, and quick decision-making are essential for success. The contrasting visual themes of white and red create a striking aesthetic that reinforces the gameplay mechanics while providing players with clear visual cues for each realm's unique properties.
